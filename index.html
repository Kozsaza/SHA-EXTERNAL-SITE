Compliance-as-a-Service Architecture

Section I

Regulatory Moat: The MSO Framework

SHA avoids Anti-Kickback Statute (AKS) violations by operating as a legitimate Management Services Organization. We do not pay "referral fees"; we operate under Personal Services and Management Contracts (42 CFR § 1001.952(d)).

  • Fixed Fair Market Value (FMV): Fees are based on technology licenses and admin services provided—never on patient volume.
  • Integrated LMS: Professionals must complete Scalp Health 101 Certification. Any direction is based on standardized triage protocols, not financial incentive.

Section II

Technical Moat: The ZK-Bridge

To remain "HIPAA-Blind," SHA utilizes a cryptographic "Proof of Referral" system. We act as a "Traffic Controller," not a data repository, keeping the company outside the legal definition of a "Business Associate."
